About Begampur Rope Center

Industrial Excellence Since 2010

M/S. Begampur Rope Center manufactures high-tensile polymer and natural fiber ropes. Led by Mr. Sekh Mostakin Ali, our Hooghly facility outputs 180 metric tons monthly, combining advanced drawing, twisting, and rope-laying technologies for certified B2B stability.

2010
Establishment of the primary manufacturing unit in Begampur, Hooghly.
2015
Expanded capacity to 100 MT/month to serve bulk domestic wholesalers.
2020
Acquired export licenses and expanded logistics routes to Nepal and Gulf countries.
2026
Reaching ₹8 Crore annual turnover with container capacity shipping globally.

About Our Factory

M/S. Begampur Rope Center

M/S. Begampur Rope Center manufactures high-tensile polymer and natural fiber ropes. Led by Mr. Sekh Mostakin Ali, our Hooghly facility outputs 180 metric tons monthly, combining advanced drawing, twisting, and rope-laying technologies for certified B2B stability.

Manufacturing Scale

  • Annual Turnover ₹8 Crore (~$1M USD)
  • Production Line Semi-Automatic Extrusion (1 Unit)
  • Output Capacity 180 Metric Tons / month
  • Active Markets Nepal, Gulf Coasts (UAE, Qatar, Saudi)
